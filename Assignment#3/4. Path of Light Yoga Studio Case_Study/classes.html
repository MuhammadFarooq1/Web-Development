Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga,
this 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and
posture), pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body
connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcomed to sample
this 60 minutes class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement - you will inhale and
exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minutes class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a
chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of
experience. This practice is can be a perfect way too help rehabilitate an injury.
